🏦 Bank & credit card spam calls — how to stop them

Register on TRAI DND: trai.gov.in or call 1909 — select category "Financial services"
SMS START DND to 1909 — blocks all commercial calls legally within 7 days
Banks legally cannot call DND-registered numbers for marketing — file a complaint at trai.gov.in/dnd-complaints if they still call
Your number got sold — this is legal in India via "consent" buried in loan/credit card application forms. Nothing you can do retroactively except DND + block.
Use the Emails tab → write to the specific bank's Data Protection Officer citing DPDP Act 2023 Section 13 demanding deletion of your marketing data
For future: never tick "I agree to receive offers" on any bank form. Use a secondary number for financial applications.

📱 Phone number exposed — what to do

Unlist from Truecaller: truecaller.com/unlisting — takes 24 hrs
Email JustDial: privacy@justdial.com requesting removal
If in a public PDF (college list, etc.) — email the institution directly, CC their IT/admin
Use a secondary SIM for app registrations going forward

📧 Email breached — what to do

Change password everywhere you reused it — use Bitwarden (free) to manage unique passwords
Enable 2FA on Gmail, banking, Instagram, LinkedIn immediately
For new signups use email aliases: SimpleLogin (free, open source)
Monitor monthly at haveibeenpwned.com
