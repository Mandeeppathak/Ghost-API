# 👻 GhostAPI — Take Back Your Digital Privacy

Ever wonder who has your data? GhostAPI is a completely free, open-source privacy tool designed to map out your digital footprint and help you erase it in minutes instead of weeks. 

The best part? It runs entirely inside your browser. **No databases, no analytics, and zero tracking — your data never leaves your computer.**

---

## ✨ What it Does

- **Instant Exposure Mapping:** Just copy and paste what Google or data breach sites show about you. The app effortlessly highlights what's exposed and grades your overall privacy risk.
- **One-Click Deletion Letters:** Stop staring at a blank screen trying to sound like a lawyer. The AI drafts rock-solid data deletion emails tailored to global privacy laws, including **India's DPDP Act 2023**, **GDPR**, and **CCPA**.
- **Pre-Built Opt-Out Checklist:** A targeted, step-by-step game plan with direct links to take your records down from major data brokers without burning any AI credits.
- **Spam Control Blueprint:** Tired of constant credit card calls and spam? Check out built-in mini-guides on triggering TRAI DND actions and recovering from leaked credentials.

---

## 🛠️ How it Works Under the Hood

GhostAPI uses a smart **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)** serverless approach. Instead of channeling your personal details through a hidden backend server, the app links directly from your browser sandbox to your chosen AI provider:

- **The UI Stack:** Handcrafted utilizing lightweight HTML5, dynamic CSS variables with a dark blue glow, and clean, native JavaScript.
- **Local Settings:** Your operational profiles and keys are securely remembered inside your personal browser `localStorage`.
- **Supported Foundations:** Take your pick! The tool supports **Anthropic Claude**, **OpenAI GPT**, **Groq**, and **Google Gemini** (featuring an automated model fallback utility).

## 🚀 Quick Start (Up & Running in 30 Seconds)

Since this app is bundled into just one self-contained web page, setup is incredibly easy:

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser.
3. Click on **Settings**, paste your developer API key (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini, or Groq), and hit **Save**.
4. Jump back to the **Scan** panel and test it out!

> 💡 **A Quick Tech Note:** Browsers usually block AI API responses when you run raw files directly from your desktop (`file://`) due to security restrictions called CORS. Hosting the file online for free completely clears this up!

## 🌐 Launching it Privately for Free

### Option 1: Netlify Drop (Fastest)
1. Go to [Netlify Drop](https://www.netlify.com/drop).
2. Simply drag and drop your `index.html` file into the box.
3. Boom! You have a secure, live web link (like `https://yourname.netlify.app`).

### Option 2: GitHub Pages
1. Push this file to a new public GitHub repository.
2. Head over to **Settings** > **Pages**.
3. Turn on deployment from your branch, save, and you are good to go.
